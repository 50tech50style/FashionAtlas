Privacy Policy for FashionAtlas

Effective Date: November 2024

Introduction

FashionAtlas ("the Extension") is designed with your privacy in mind. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our Google Chrome extension. We take your privacy seriously and have structured FashionAtlas so that no data is collected, transmitted, or stored externally. All data stays on your device and is only accessible to you.

1. Information Handling and Collection

1.1 Image Data

Usage: When you analyze an image for color and fabric details, the pixel data from the image is processed locally within your browser.

Storage: Image data is not stored or shared. The analysis occurs in real-time, and the data is discarded immediately after the operation.

1.2 User Preferences and Settings

Storage: FashionAtlas may use Chrome’s storage API to save your preferences, such as your selected color season or analysis history. This data is stored locally on your device and is not accessible to anyone other than you.

1.3 Interaction Data

Usage: Any data related to your interactions, such as clicks on images, is processed locally to enable the Extension’s features. This information is not stored or shared beyond the current session.

2. Data Sharing

FashionAtlas does not share, sell, or transmit any data to external parties. Since the Extension does not collect or store any personal or usage data, there is nothing to share.

3. Data Security

FashionAtlas processes all data locally on your device. We do not transmit any information over the internet, so there is no risk of your data being accessed or compromised by third parties through our Extension. Your data remains within your control at all times.

4. User Control and Data Management

You have full control over your data while using FashionAtlas:

  • Local Storage Management: You can clear your browser’s local storage if you wish to remove any saved settings or preferences.
  • Uninstallation: Uninstalling the Extension will remove all locally stored data related to FashionAtlas.

5. Children’s Privacy

FashionAtlas does not knowingly collect or process any data from children. Since no personal information is collected or transmitted, the Extension is safe for use by individuals of all ages.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if there are changes to the Extension’s functionality or privacy practices. Any updates will be posted on the Chrome Web Store, and the "Effective Date" at the top will reflect the date of the latest changes.
